**Palette**: Flat coral, sage, mustard, sky blue — all mid-tone, slightly desaturated

**Type**: Clean geometric sans (TT Norms, DM Sans family); neutral brand personality

**Texture**: Flat illustration; no shadows; no depth — 100% flat fills

**Shape**: Abstract human figures with no facial features; mismatched skin-tone blobs as diversity signal

**Motion**: Gentle loop; minimal and pleasant

**Spatial**: Generous whitespace; single hero illustration

**Cultural markers**: Racially ambiguous blob figures, single-leaf plant, geometric face-less humans

**Non-negotiables**: flat illustration + blob figures + desaturated corporate palette

**Fatigue note**: HIGH — peaked ~2019–2021; now reads as generic SaaS; active avoidance often better than adoption

**Connotation**: Approachable startup optimism and safe playfulness; friendly and illustrative, but often generic when overused.
