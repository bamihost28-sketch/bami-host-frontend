---
slug: y2k
label: Y2K
family: digital-internet-native
era: 1997–2003
aliases: ["Y2K Futurism", "Cyber Y2K"]
---

**Palette**: Chrome silver, iridescent holographic gradients, acid green / cyan / hot pink / magenta on white or silver

**Type**: Rounded bubbly sans-serif (Eurostile, inflated letterforms), mixed-case, heavy outlines

**Texture**: Metallic sheen, plastic gloss, translucent jelly surfaces, lenticular effects

**Shape**: Inflated pill shapes, beveled 3D orbs, extreme rounded-rectangles

**Motion**: Spring-elastic — pop-in with bounce; cursor trails; interface chrome animates

**Spatial**: Dense and layered; high visual complexity; multiple panes

**Cultural markers**: CRT monitors, loading bars, starfield backgrounds, "under construction" GIFs, holographic sticker language

**Non-negotiables**: metallic/chrome surface + bubbly geometry + acid/iridescent color

**Connotation**: Nostalgia + camp + Gen-Z reclamation; ironic warmth, not earnest futurism
