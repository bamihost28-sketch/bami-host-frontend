**Palette**: Sepia, warm brown, charcoal, forest green, deep burgundy, aged cream — all desaturated, candlelit

**Type**: Old-style serifs (Garamond, Playfair Display, EB Garamond); high-contrast thick/thin strokes

**Texture**: Aged paper, book cloth, leather, wood paneling — analog surfaces with patina

**Shape**: Classical architectural motifs (arches, columns); bookshelf grids; symmetrical institutional forms

**Motion**: Slow and deliberate — page turns, slow fades; no kinetic energy

**Spatial**: Dense and scholarly — text-heavy; no excessive whitespace; library density

**Cultural markers**: Open books, quill/fountain pen, candles, ivy, telescopes, globes, classical sculpture

**Non-negotiables**: sepia/warm-dark palette + old-style serif + aged analog texture

**Connotation**: Scholarly melancholy and old-world seriousness; romantic, bookish, and secretive rather than bright or casual.
