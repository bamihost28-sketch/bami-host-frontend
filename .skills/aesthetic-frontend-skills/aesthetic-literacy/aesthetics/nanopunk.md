**Palette**: surgical white, carbon black, oil-slick iridescence, electric cyan, bio-lime.

**Type**: ultra-light molecular/lab labels and tiny numerics.

**Texture**: particles, mesh, mist, programmable surfaces, cellular translucency.

**Shape**: swarms, tessellated skins, liquid-metal beads, filament webs.

**Motion**: flocking, accretion, disintegration, repair, replication.

**Spatial**: fields, clouds, chambers, body interiors; scale ambiguity.

**Cultural markers**: nanites, programmable matter, grey-goo dread, Diamond Age lineage.

**Non-negotiables**: surgical white, carbon black, oil-slick iridescence, electric cyan, bio-lime + particles, mesh, mist, programmable surfaces, cellular translucency + swarms, tessellated skins, liquid-metal beads, filament webs + fields, clouds, chambers, body interiors; scale ambiguity.

## Connotation

Nanopunk carries a specific robotics/speculative-technology connotation; use the source-backed cues above as a system rather than as isolated moodboard decoration.

## Scope

Use when the product, interface, or visual artifact benefits from this exact machine/body/worldbuilding language. Avoid when a neighboring aesthetic would communicate the intent with fewer cultural or accessibility risks.

## Related / Subsets

Adjacent to cyberpunk and organic-digital, but defined by swarm/programmed-matter behavior and microscopic scale rather than city grime or soft blobs.
