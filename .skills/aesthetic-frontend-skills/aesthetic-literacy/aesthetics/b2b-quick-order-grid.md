**Palette**: White/grey base with sparse action blue, validation red/green, muted quantity cells, and upload affordance grey (`#FFFFFF`, `#F3F4F6`, `#2563EB`, `#DC2626`, `#16A34A`, `#6B7280`).

**Type**: Practical sans, tabular SKU strings, small metadata text, bold row totals, and clear form labels. Monospace can be used for SKU codes but body copy remains plain sans.

**Texture**: Form fields, row dividers, quantity boxes, upload zones, autocomplete menus, CSV chips, validation marks, and compact product thumbnails. Texture is input repetition.

**Shape**: Thin inputs, table rows, inline action icons, restrained buttons, upload cards, and validation badges. Corners are modest (`4–8px`); large hero shapes are inappropriate.

**Motion**: Autocomplete, row insertion, validation nudges, quantity steppers, upload confirmations, and reorder list expansion. Motion is quick and work-preserving (`100–220ms`).

**Spatial**: Long vertical lists, quick-entry modules, side-by-side quantity editing, sticky cart totals, and high line-item density. Browsing gives way to known-item entry.

**Cultural markers**: SKU fields, requisition lists, CSV upload, purchase-order language, product-name autocomplete, case packs, quantity breaks, reorder templates, account pricing.

**Non-negotiables**: known-item line-entry workflow + table/input dominant layout + validation-first interaction design + low-ornament speed emphasis. If these are removed, the result becomes a generic adjacent style rather than B2B Quick-Order Grid.

## Connotation

**Authentic B2B utility**. It signals procurement efficiency and replenishment expertise, not consumer product discovery.

## Scope

Suitable for wholesale order pads, procurement portals, repeat purchases, parts catalogs, and inventory replenishment. Not suitable for inspiration-led retail or premium storefront storytelling.

**Related / Subsets**: Distinct from ordinary ecommerce: known-item replenishment replaces browsing and product seduction. Distinct from generic admin UI because SKU-first order entry is the center of gravity.
