**Type**: Variable-weight rounded humanist sans; fluid; personality-forward

**Texture**: SVG blob shapes with soft edges; gradient mesh; glass morphism influence

**Shape**: Asymmetric amoeba forms; no hard angles; squircles and blobs

**Motion**: Fluid morphing; breathing; physics-based soft-body animation

**Spatial**: Generous; blobs as compositional elements rather than content containers

**Cultural markers**: Gradient blob hero sections, floating orb UI, Stripe/Figma landing page aesthetic

**Non-negotiables**: blob/amoeba shapes + fluid gradient fills + organic movement

**Connotation**: Biomorphic technology and soft futurism; adaptive, humane, and nature-coded rather than mechanical.
