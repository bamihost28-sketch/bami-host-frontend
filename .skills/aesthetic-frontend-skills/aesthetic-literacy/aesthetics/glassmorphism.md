**Palette**: Pastel or vibrant blurs; colorful gradient backgrounds visible through frosted glass panels

**Type**: Clean sans-serif on frosted surfaces; often white or very light

**Texture**: `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`; frosted glass panels; soft inner border glow

**Shape**: Rounded panels (16–24px border-radius typical); floating cards

**Motion**: Smooth sliding; parallel z-depth reveals

**Spatial**: Layered z-depth; foreground glass over colorful background

**Cultural markers**: macOS Big Sur, Windows 11, Stripe UI influence

**Non-negotiables**: blur transparency + colorful background visible through panel

**Fatigue note**: MODERATE — still functional but no longer surprising

**Connotation**: Premium digital translucency and ambient depth; airy, futuristic, and app-native when contrast remains controlled.
