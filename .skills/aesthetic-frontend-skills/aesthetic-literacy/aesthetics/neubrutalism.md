**Palette**: Vivid primary yellows, electric blues, stark black borders — high saturation

**Type**: Bold grotesks; high contrast weight; brutal presence

**Texture**: Hard box shadows (no blur); solid-color fills; thick solid borders

**Shape**: Hard corners OR exaggerated rounded corners with thick border; no subtlety

**Motion**: Bold snap transitions; thick border elements; deliberate attention-demanding

**Spatial**: Dense; borders define all boundaries; no whitespace softness

**Cultural markers**: Hard solid-drop-shadow cards, thick 3–4px borders, bold block-color buttons

**Non-negotiables**: thick visible borders + hard/no border-radius + primary-dominant palette

**Connotation**: Deliberate anti-polish and internet-native confidence; loud, direct, and playful rather than refined.
