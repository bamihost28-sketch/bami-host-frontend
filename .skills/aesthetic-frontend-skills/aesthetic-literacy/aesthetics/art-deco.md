**Palette**: Black, gold/champagne, ivory/cream; rich jewel tones (sapphire, emerald, ruby) as accent

**Type**: Geometric slab or condensed display — strong vertical rhythm; chevron-inspired letterforms (Broadway, Deco geometric)

**Texture**: Luxury materials — lacquer, chrome, marble, ebony; highly polished surfaces

**Shape**: Geometric sunbursts, chevrons, zigzags, stepped pyramids; strong bilateral symmetry

**Motion**: Stately and monumental — no playfulness; deliberate pace

**Spatial**: Monumental symmetry; strong vertical axis; architectural scale

**Cultural markers**: Sunburst, stepped pyramid, peacock-fan geometry, jazz-age figures, skyscraper ornament

**Non-negotiables**: geometric luxury shapes + strong symmetry + gold/black palette

**Connotation**: Premium glamour and civic monumentality; confident, ornamental, and aspirational rather than cozy or improvised.
