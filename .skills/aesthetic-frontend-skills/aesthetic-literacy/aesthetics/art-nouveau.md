**Palette**: Organic earthy-to-jewel: muted ochre, sage, burgundy, peacock blue; gold as accent

**Type**: Flowing organic letterforms integrated into composition; sinuous custom display typefaces

**Texture**: Sinuous flat linework; natural patterns — lily pad, peacock feather, root systems

**Shape**: Organic flowing curves; female form and nature as structural elements; whiplash curve

**Motion**: Flowing; sinuous movement implied in static composition

**Spatial**: Decorative borders as compositional frame; pattern fills all space

**Cultural markers**: Mucha maidens, lily pad ornament, peacock feather, flowing hair integrated with floral, gilded frame language

**Non-negotiables**: sinuous organic line + nature-as-structure + flowing figure-ground integration

**Connotation**: Romantic craft luxury with organic sophistication; sensuous and botanical, not minimal or machine-neutral.
