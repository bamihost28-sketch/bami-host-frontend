**Palette**: Hot red, bright yellow, royal blue, cobalt, black, cream/aged paper, emerald, carnival pink, and multicolour fields.

**Type**: Stacked display lettering, condensed capitals, slab/deco/showcard forms, aggressive hierarchy, outlined type, and dense act listings.

**Texture**: Paper wear, paste-up seams, ink variance, letterpress/offset roughness, sun-faded color, overprint mismatch, and cheap-paper grain.

**Shape**: Vertical broadsides, medallions, starbursts, scrolls, badges, panel dividers, side columns, banners, and overprint fields.

**Motion**: Barked excitement, snap-in emphasis, implied performer action, upward thrust, carousel repetition, and kinetic type angles.

**Spatial**: Maximal information density with headline, spectacle image, act copy, dates, showman branding, sidebars, and strong borders.

**Cultural markers**: Clowns, animal acts, equestrian acts, trapeze, novelty acts, city/date overprints, showman rhetoric, touring entertainment, and fairground ephemera.

**Non-negotiables**: sensational display type + dense hierarchical copy + bright poster palette + archival print wear + entertainment broadside logic. If these are removed, the result collapses into an adjacent or generic style rather than Fairground / Carnival Poster Art.
