**Palette**: black, bone, gunmetal, bruise-purple, toxic green, dried-blood red.

**Type**: narrow technical sans, sterile labels, specimen numerics.

**Texture**: flesh-metal fusion, tendons, tubing, ribbed exoskeletons, wet gloss.

**Shape**: spines, rib cages, vertebral arcs, pods, incubators, asymmetry.

**Motion**: twitch, pulse, respiration, servo-spasm, tendon-like extension.

**Spatial**: labs, vats, operating rooms, biotech corridors, body assembly.

**Cultural markers**: H. R. Giger, Alien, Ghost in the Shell, body horror.

**Non-negotiables**: black, bone, gunmetal, bruise-purple, toxic green, dried-blood red + flesh-metal fusion, tendons, tubing, ribbed exoskeletons, wet gloss + spines, rib cages, vertebral arcs, pods, incubators, asymmetry + labs, vats, operating rooms, biotech corridors, body assembly.
