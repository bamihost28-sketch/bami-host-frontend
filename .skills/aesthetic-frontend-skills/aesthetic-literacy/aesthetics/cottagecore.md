**Palette**: Warm cream, sage green, dusty rose, terracotta, aged linen — all low-saturation

**Type**: Botanical script; serif with ink-press texture; handwritten notes aesthetic

**Texture**: Linen, watercolor wash, botanical illustration print, aged paper

**Shape**: Rounded and irregular — organic, not geometric; florals and botanicals

**Motion**: Gentle and unhurried; slow fade; nothing snappy

**Spatial**: Abundant but curated — every surface filled warmly, like a cottage shelf

**Cultural markers**: Pressed flowers, hand-lettered recipes, mushrooms, bees, overflowing bookshelves, candlelight

**Non-negotiables**: warm organic palette + botanical/natural motifs + handcrafted texture

**Connotation**: Pastoral comfort and handmade nostalgia; gentle, domestic, and escapist rather than urban or high-tech.
