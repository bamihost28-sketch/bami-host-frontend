**Palette**: Black, white, and one systematic primary; red or black on white dominates

**Type**: Grotesk sans-serif (Helvetica, Akzidenz-Grotesk, Univers) — objective, neutral, no personality

**Texture**: Flat; no decoration; pure information

**Shape**: Mathematical grid; no organic curves; rectilinear absolute discipline

**Motion**: None by design principle; information over animation

**Spatial**: Mathematical whitespace; grid proportion is content

**Cultural markers**: Müller-Brockmann grid, photographic halftone, objective text hierarchy, no decoration

**Non-negotiables**: neutral grotesk type + mathematical grid + objective flat presentation

**Connotation**: Institutional clarity and typographic authority; neutral, precise, and trustworthy rather than expressive.
